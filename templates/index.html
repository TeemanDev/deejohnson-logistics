Real-time Tracking

Know where your package is at all times
What is this service?

Every package shipped with DeeJohnson Logistics receives a unique tracking code. You can use this code on our website to see exactly where your package is, what stage it's at, and when to expect delivery.

How tracking works
  1. Receive tracking code via email, SMS, or WhatsApp after shipment
  2. Enter code on our tracking page at deejohnson-logistics.com/track
  3. See real-time updates on your package location and status
  4. Track internationally with partner courier after customs
What information will I see?
  • Current location - Where your package is right now
  • Status updates - Picked up, in transit, customs clearance, out for delivery
  • Timestamps - Date and time of each update
  • Estimated delivery date - When to expect arrival
  • Partner tracking link - Continue tracking with FedEx/DHL after international handoff
Get notifications
  • Email updates - Receive automatic status updates
  • SMS alerts - Text message notifications (available on request)
  • WhatsApp notifications - Get updates via WhatsApp
Note: Once your package leaves Nigeria, you will receive a partner tracking code (FedEx, DHL, UPS, Royal Mail, or DPD UK) to continue tracking internationally.

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers. Here's what our customers usually ask us.

Shipping & Delivery

How long does delivery take from Ibadan to Lagos?

Standard delivery from Ibadan to Lagos takes 1-2 business days. Express delivery is available within 24 hours for urgent shipments.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes! We ship to over 200 countries worldwide through our partnership with FedEx, DHL, and UPS. International delivery typically takes 5-7 business days.

How can I track my package from Ibadan to Lagos?

You can track your package by entering our local DeeJohnson tracking code (e.g., DJL-2024-XXXXX) on our Track Page. You'll receive real-time updates on your shipment's location and status.

What happens if my package is delayed?

If your package is delayed due to customs or other factors, we will notify you immediately via email or SMS. Our customer support team will keep you updated on the status and provide an estimated delivery date.

Pricing & Payments

How much does shipping cost?

Shipping costs vary based on package weight, dimensions, and destination. Contact us for a free quote, or use our shipping calculator (coming soon) to get an instant estimate.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank transfers, cash on delivery (COD), and online payments via Paystack. International payments can be made via wire transfer or PayPal.

Do I need to pay customs duties?

Customs duties and taxes are the responsibility of the recipient. However, we provide full customs clearance services and will inform you of any applicable duties before delivery.

Are there any hidden fees?

No hidden fees! We provide transparent pricing with a detailed breakdown of all costs including shipping, handling, customs clearance, and insurance (if applicable).

Package Information

What items are restricted or prohibited?

We cannot ship hazardous materials, flammable items, weapons, illegal drugs, perishable goods without proper packaging, or items prohibited by Nigerian customs. Contact us for a complete list of restricted items.

How should I package my items?

Use sturdy boxes with proper cushioning material (bubble wrap, foam, or packing peanuts). Seal all seams with strong packing tape. For fragile items, mark "FRAGILE" on the box. We also offer professional packaging services.

Is my package insured?

We offer optional insurance coverage for valuable shipments. Insurance covers loss or damage during transit. Contact us to add insurance to your shipment.

What if my package is damaged?

If your package arrives damaged, please document it with photos and contact us within 24 hours. We'll initiate a damage claim and work with our insurance provider to compensate you.
